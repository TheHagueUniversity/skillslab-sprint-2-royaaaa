About me

My name is Roya, and this website works both as my portfolio and my 'escape' place where I can look for inspiration whenever I lose the motivation. I come from a small town in Uzbekistan. I live in The Hague with my little sister and little brother and my mom. When I was around the age of 8 we moved to the Netherlands. I went to Hofstad Lyceum, VWO Bilingual. After the first 3 years, my Bilingual education changed to IB. I received my International Baccalaureate diploma in 2015 and got my VWO diploma in 2016. I study Communication and Multimedia Design User Experience on The Hague University of Applied Science.

My interest varies from psychology, human interest and behaviour to design and creativity. I love to explore Photoshop and making graphic designs, explore my creativity. I find human interaction and behaviour very interesting

In my free time I like to read and shop (obviously). But I also like taking long walks on the beach or whenever and having a chill day in. I like working with Photoshop the most and I've been using Photoshop CS6 for my graphic designs for quite a while now.
