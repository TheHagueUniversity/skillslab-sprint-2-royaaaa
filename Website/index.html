Designing with your mind

Art is establishing your ideas. Art is coping with things. Art is letting your imaginations go free. Art is creativity. Creativity is exploring and pushing yourself to the max. This all is crucial for one thing: designing. Even as a kid I loved exploring art and creativity. When I was 12 years old I was introduced to Photoshop. Now, at 21 I still love using PS and I still love learning more amazing things I can do with it. My creativity has helped me a lot whether it was to feel better more secure about myself or take my mind off of things.

My name is Roya, and this website works both as my portfolio and my 'escape' place where I can look for inspiration whenever I lose the motivation. Feel free to look through the site.
